• How do I create an appointment?
    Go to View Calendar. Click and hold a day (not in the past). The add appointment dialog should appear.

    You can also click on Week (top right). Hold and drag to select the appointment's duration.
  • How do I view an appointment's details or delete it?
    Go to View Calendar. Click on a specific event. You can then see its details and delete it.
  • How do I prevent certain days in the week from being registered?
    Go to Edit Calendar. Completely remove all the time ranges for a day by clicking the remove button until there are no more time ranges.
  • How do I change my language?
    Go to Edit Business, then under Which language would you like to use?, select a different language.
  • What is the QR code for?
    Your clients will be able to scan your QR code to instantly land on your business page. You can print this image out and make it easier for your clients to scan your code.
