Privacy Policy

Last revision: Aug 16, 2018

Information is shared to us when:

  1. Signing in with Google

    • Google will share your email address with us but not your password or any other personal information.

    • Your email address is used for the sole purpose of signing in.

    • We will never share your email address or send you any emails.

  2. Signing in with Facebook

    • Facebook will share an ID with us but not your password or any other personal information.

    • This ID does not let us find your facebook profile, and your name is not shared to us either.

    • Learn more about app-scoped IDs on facebook (developers.facebook.com/docs/facebook-login/connecting-accounts).

  3. Saving your business information

    • On app.bookwith.biz/edit_business, you can provide us with your business information.

    • This information is only shown on your business page (your-subdomain.bookwith.biz).

    • We will never share any information provided on any other page or with third parties.

    • Your profile picture is uploaded to imgur and follows their privacy policy (imgur.com/privacy).

  4. When appointments are made

    • Your customers provide their name and phone number when making an appointment.

    • We will never share their name or phone number with anyone except with you.

Our usage of Cookies:

  1. Login and Session

    • We use cookies to keep you logged into our website.

  2. Imgur

    • Imgur may use cookies, and follows their privacy policy (imgur.com/privacy).

Data Protection

We store data using Google datastore. (cloud.google.com/terms/data-processing-terms)
